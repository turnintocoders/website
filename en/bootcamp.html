Bootcamp

The bootcamp is a super intensive training course in a limited amount of time, and when you are done you've learnt a job and you can dive in the market of web developers.

8 intensive weeks

The heart of the bootcamp is formed by 8 intensive weeks in which you'll attend workshops and build projects, everything in 4 groups of 4 people, always in a cooperative way.

During the course you'll be exposed to full-stack development in JavaScript, test-driven development, relational databases. We'll also touch concepts of UX design and project management.

You can find more details about the content of the bootcamp in this Github repository.

Example week schedule

  • Monday: 10-12 workshop, 15-17 workshop, group study
  • Tuesday: 10-12: workshop, project introduction and group work
  • Wednesday: group work on the project
  • Thursday: project, 15-17: code review with external mentors
  • Friday: project, 15-17: group presentation of the projects, meeting with companies and externals

Projects & mentorship

Once the bootcamp is over you can test yourself developing a project for 4 weeks, followed by 8 weeks of mentorship, in which you'll have the opportunity of creating your own curriculum building projects, meeting companies or creating your own startup!

Prerequisites

Before attending the bootcamp you need to have followed a path on your own. That will be useful also in understanding if you are really interested in becoming a web developer and it will allow you to reach a skill level that will be as omogeneous as possible with the other students.

The prerequisites are:

  1. Register on Github
  2. Join our chat on Gitter and actively participate
  3. Register on FreeCodeCamp and complete the sections:
    • HTML5 & CSS
    • Basic JavaScript
    • Object Oriented and Functional Programming
    • Basic Algorithm Scripting
  4. When you are confident enough, build a one page website using Github pages
  5. Register on Codewars and, in JavaScript:
    • Reach 5th kyu
    • Create one kata

To avoid making the achievements of the prerequisites too frustrating or long, you can join us at the weekly meetups where we can help you finishing them.

Pre-bootcamp

You can work alone on the prerequisites or you can join the 4 weeks long pre-bootcamp, just before the bootcamp.

In the pre-bootcamp we work together on the prerequisites, plus we have workshops, projects and peronal work. The pre-bootcamp is just a way to complete the prerequisites. You can do that alone, but consider it as a help and a faster, more focused, way to work on the prerequisites.

Pre-bootcamp example week schedule

  • Monday: 10-12 workshop, personal study
  • Tuesday: 10-12: workshop, project introduction and personal study
  • Wednesday: project
  • Thursday: project, 15-17: code review
  • Friday: project, group presentation of the projects, meeting with companies and externals

The rythm is a bit kinder compared to the bootcamp and the path is a bit more indipendent, but you can already taste how the following weeks will be like, in full autonomy.
