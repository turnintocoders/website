Bootcamp

8 weeks of super intensive training to become a JavaScript full-stack web developer and jumpstart your career in an industry growing at full speed.

The programme is composed by

Pre-bootcamp. 4 optional weeks to work together on the prerequisites with workshops and personal work. Start building up momentum for the bootcamp and build up your skills. Working on the prerequisites is also a way for you to undersand if you really like to become a developer and to reach an homogeneous level with the other participants.

8 intensive weeks. The heart of the bootcamp is formed by 8 intensive weeks with workshops and projects, working in groups of 4 people in a cooperative way.

The course is about full-stack JavaScript development, test driven development, relational databases and team work, with a bit of UX design and project management. You can find all the details of the bootcamp and the week schedule in the Github repository.

Project. 4 weeks after the bootcamp where you can test yourself developing a project, together with your team.

Mentorship. Up to 8 optional weeks in which you can create your own curriculum building projects, meeting companies or creating your own startup!

Coding for everyone. To meet the mentors and to taste what the bootcamp will be you can join our weekly meetups.
