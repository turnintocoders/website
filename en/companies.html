Companies

Does your company have needs concering web developing? We train new coders and let them find their first job in the field. Hire a junior developer for your team or company.

Here are some things we can do for you:

  • Train your employees during the bootcamp: let you company boost its competencies and jump forward. 8 weeks of high-intensive training, and they’ll be back as ninjas.
  • We will be happy to introduce you to our skilled coders, during the bootcamp and afterwards. You’ll see how good we are in developing projects in team. If you hire them, you’ll also gain their community support in case of need.
  • Let us train your employees for a specific techonlogy in front-end or back-end development. We organize mini-bootcamps several times per year, on any technology you would need. We can also arrange something different, tailored for your needs.
  • If you want to show practical support to advanced digital literacy, join other companies on our supporters area, on our website.
  • Coming soon: let our graduated students work on some projects for you.
